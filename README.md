Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

Overview
This project presents an interactive Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard built to analyze coffee shop sales performance across years, months, weekdays, product categories, and cities. The dashboard helps track overall business performance through key sales metrics and visual insights.

Objective
The main objective of this project is to analyze coffee shop sales data and build a dashboard that provides insights into:

* overall sales performance
* revenue and profit trends
* category-wise sales contribution
* city-wise revenue distribution
* weekday transaction patterns
* monthly and yearly sales comparison

KPIs

* Total Transactions: 9,971
* Total Quantity Sold: 14,117
* Total Revenue: $46,374
* Total Profit: $16,231

Dashboard Features

* KPI cards for Transactions, Quantity Sold, Revenue, and Profit
* Revenue by Category analysis
* Category Transactions comparison
* Weekday Transactions trend analysis
* Yearly Revenue % distribution
* Monthly Transactions comparison
* Revenue % by City analysis
* Monthly Revenue & Profit comparison
* Interactive filters for Year, Month, and City

Dashboard Analysis

1. KPI Summary
   The dashboard starts with KPI cards showing the overall business performance:

* Total Transactions
* Total Quantity Sold
* Total Revenue
* Total Profit

2. Revenue by Category
   This section shows revenue generated by different product categories such as:

* Tea Beverages
* Specialty Drinks
* Snacks
* Sandwiches
* Desserts
* Cold Drinks
* Coffee Beverages

3. Category Transactions
   This visual compares transaction contribution across product categories and helps identify which categories have higher customer demand.

4. Weekday Transactions
   This chart shows transaction trends across all weekdays to understand customer activity and peak business days.

5. Yearly Revenue Percentage
   This visual compares revenue contribution across the years 2021, 2022, and 2023.

6. Monthly Transactions
   This section compares monthly transaction counts across different years to identify trends and seasonal sales patterns.

7. Revenue by City
   This chart shows revenue contribution from different cities such as:

* Austin
* Chicago
* Los Angeles
* New York
* Seattle

8. Monthly Revenue and Profit
   This visual compares monthly revenue and profit to track financial performance and profitability over time.

Key Insights

* The dashboard provides a complete view of sales, revenue, profit, and transactions in one place.
* It helps identify top-performing product categories.
* It shows weekday transaction patterns to understand peak sales days.
* It compares monthly revenue and profit for performance tracking.
* It highlights city-wise revenue contribution for location-based analysis.
* It provides year-wise revenue comparison to understand long-term performance.
